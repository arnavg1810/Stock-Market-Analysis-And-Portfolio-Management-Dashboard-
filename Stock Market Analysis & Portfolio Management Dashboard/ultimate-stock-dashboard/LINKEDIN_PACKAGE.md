## 1️⃣ LinkedIn Post (≈200 words)

**Stock Market Analysis & Portfolio Optimization — End-to-End Finance Project**

I built a full-stack financial analytics dashboard that lets users analyze any stock ticker (Indian or global), not just predefined ones.

**What it does:**
• **Dynamic ticker selection** — Enter any valid Yahoo Finance symbol (e.g. RELIANCE.NS, TCS.NS, AAPL, ^NSEI) and get 5 years of historical data.
• **Jupyter notebook** — End-to-end analysis: data download, cleaning, EDA, trend analysis (50/200 MA), returns, volatility, max drawdown, Beta, Sharpe ratio, and Monte Carlo portfolio optimization (10,000 portfolios) with efficient frontier and max Sharpe / min volatility weights.
• **Streamlit web app** — Interactive dashboard with price & moving average charts, key metrics (return, volatility, Sharpe, drawdown), rolling volatility, correlation heatmap, portfolio optimization (efficient frontier + pie chart of optimal allocation), and investment growth comparison.

**Tech:** Python, yfinance, pandas, numpy, matplotlib, Streamlit, Plotly.

**Concepts:** Modern Portfolio Theory, Sharpe ratio, Beta, volatility, maximum drawdown, efficient frontier, diversification.

The system is scalable and production-style: clean structure, requirements.txt, README, and finance-level interpretation (executive summary, risk–return, investor suitability). Perfect for demonstrating data + finance skills in quant or analytics roles.

If you’re into markets, Python, or building dashboards, check out the repo and try your own tickers.
